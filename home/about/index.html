ABOUT
Hailing from Providence, Rhode Island, Eli Lederberg is a graduate of Parsons School of Design, where he majored in Communication Design and minored in Film Production.
Based out of New York City, Eli primarily creates work focused in the world of film, while maintaining a deep passion for graphic design. Eli specifically focuses on the post-production process, and recently finished working on the first season of the Apple TV+ show Severance. He is currently working as a Post-Production Coordinator on the upcoming Showtime television series Let The Right One In.
